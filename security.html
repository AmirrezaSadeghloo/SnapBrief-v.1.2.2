Security & Privacy

Built for professionals under NDA. Your data is protected, always.

No Data Retention

Files are processed statelessly and never stored in a database.

All uploads and outputs are auto-deleted within 60 minutes by design.

End-to-End Encryption

Encrypted in transit (TLS 1.2+) and at rest during temporary storage.

Using HTTPS and optionally server-side encryption (e.g. AWS S3).

Enterprise-Grade LLM Privacy

SnapBrief uses OpenAI API with data logging disabled — your data is not used to train the model.

Future option: plug in your own OpenAI API key or use an on-premise model (Azure, Mistral, etc.).

Compliance & Transparency

SnapBrief is GDPR-aligned. A DPA is available upon request.

Your data remains yours. We do not claim ownership of input or output content.

“We treat your data like your top client would want us to.”
